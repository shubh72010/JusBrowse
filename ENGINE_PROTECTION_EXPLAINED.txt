**Strategy: Deterministic Spoofing (Persona-Based)**
The JusFake Engine (powered by Priv8 and RL Engines) completely replaces your real device signals with a predefined "Fake Identity" or Persona.

- **Flattening (Priv8)**: Real device data is first "pushed" into realistic generic buckets (e.g., rounding ultra-high-res screen dimensions to standard ones).
- **Glowing (RLEngine)**: The flattened data is then "mapped" to a specific Digital Persona. If your persona is a "Chrome on Windows" user, the engine ensures all JS calls return values consistent with that identity.
- **Hardware Spoofing**: Spoofs User-Agent, Platform, CPU cores, RAM, and even Screen resolution to match the chosen persona.
- **Best For**: Users who want to hide among a specific crowd or appear as a completely different device.
